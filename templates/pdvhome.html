Priority Datasets

Explore the datasets identified by EU Member States and EFTA countries as priority data sets for environmental reporting. You can filter by environmental domain, environmental legislation and country.

The list of priority datasets has been defined and is being further developed in the framework of the INSPIRE Maintenance and Implementation Work Plan (MIWP) 2016-2020.
